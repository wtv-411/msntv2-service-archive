To view your online billing statement, follow these steps:
  1. On the My billing account page, choose View my monthly statement.
  2. If you have more than one credit card, select the credit card whose monthly statement you want to view, and then choose Continue.
Notes
  • Choose Details next to your subscription's line items to view usage events such as dial-up access. Only subscriptions with usage events will display line items.
  • Choose Select a different payment method to view details for another credit card.
