Improve your connection speed
To help improve your connection speed, choose all the local MSN access numbers available from the MSN Access Numbers page (make sure they are local and not toll numbers).

To select access numbers:

  1. Choose Settings from the sign-in page.
  2. Select Choose connection settings.
  3. Choose MSN TV access numbers.
  4. You now have a few ways to try and improve your connection rate:
    • If your Connection History shows that a particular number is connecting at a slow rate, you can deselect that number (remove the checkmark).
    • You can select additional local numbers from the list.
    • If there are no additional numbers to choose from, scroll to the bottom of the page and choose Get more access numbers.
  5. Select some additional access numbers, and then choose Connect and see if your connection speed improves.
