Get the right time
Is your time off by 1 hour or more? You could be listed in the wrong time zone. To check your time zone settings:
  1. From the home page, choose Settings from the top of the page, or from the scrolling list at the bottom of the page.
  2. Choose Time zone settings.
  3. To change the current time zone, select the box next to "Time Zone:" and choose the correct time zone for your location.
  4. If applicable, make sure "Adjust clock for daylight saving changes" is checked.
  5. Choose Save Changes.
