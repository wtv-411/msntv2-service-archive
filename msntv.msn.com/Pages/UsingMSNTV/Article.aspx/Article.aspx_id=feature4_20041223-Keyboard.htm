Need help with your keyboard?
Have you been experiencing problems with your keyboard lately? If you're having problems with missing keystrokes, or if the "Q" displays as a "W," first try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Make sure your keyboard is pointing directly at the MSN TV 2 Player.
  2. Make sure there is nothing that could obstruct the signal between the keyboard and your MSN TV 2 Player (for example, a coffee table, etc.).
  3. Try replacing your batteries.

If you are still having problems, call Customer Service at 1-866-466-7688 for help. A customer service representative will be happy to help you resolve your keyboard issues.
